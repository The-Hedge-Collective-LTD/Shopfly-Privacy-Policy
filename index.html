Privacy Policy for Shopfly

Effective Date0:October 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Shopfly collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Shopify app.

1. Introduction

Shopfly provides product listing optimization services to merchants who use Shopify to power their stores. This Privacy Policy describes how personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install or use the App in connection with your Shopify store.

2. Information We Collect

When you install the App, we are automatically able to access certain types of information from your Shopify account, in accordance with Shopify's API permissions model, including:

  • Store name
  • Product listings and related metadata (titles, descriptions, tags, SEO fields, etc.)
  • Store domain

We do not collect or process payment information, customer data, or order information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve the App's functionality, specifically to:

  • Analyze and optimize your product listings
  • Suggest improvements for titles, descriptions, and SEO metadata
  • Apply updates to your store's product listings as instructed by you
  • Respond to your customer support requests

We do not use your information for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Sharing Your Information

  • With Shopify: We share information with Shopify only to the extent necessary to provide our services and comply with Shopify's policies.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may use third-party services (such as product data scraping APIs) to generate optimization suggestions. These providers are given only the data needed to perform their task and are required to protect your information.
  • No sale or rental of information: We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your store and product data only as long as your store has the App installed or as needed to comply with legal obligations. On uninstallation, we remove store-identifying information from our systems within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

If you are a Shopify merchant, you have the right to access and update the personal information we hold about your store. You may also request deletion of your data by uninstalling the App.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date."
