Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 22, 2025

At The Hedge Collective (“we,” “our,” or “us”), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Shopify app ShopFly.

1. Information We Collect

When you install or use our Shopify app, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Shop Information: Your shop name, domain, contact email, and plan type.
  • Customer & Product Data: Limited store data required to provide app functionality, such as product titles, descriptions, prices, and analytics data.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app, such as feature usage, to improve performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our app’s functionality.
  • Offer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • Enhance the user experience through analytics and updates.
  • Comply with Shopify’s and legal requirements.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data. We may share information with trusted service providers only when necessary to operate the app (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics). All third parties are required to safeguard your data in compliance with applicable laws.

4. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at support@thehedgecollective.com.

7. Shopify Integration

Our app integrates with the Shopify platform. We comply with Shopify’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We do not access or store customer payment data.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this page, and the “Effective Date” will be updated accordingly.
